My Favourite Movies?

I don’t necessarily watch movies that often (ik, average gen A kid) but I still have some movies that I have particularly enjoyed.

All Quiet on the Western Front
I personally enjoyed this movie because I enjoyed its depiction of the First World War and wars in general. It refuses to add any sort of cliche or stereotype about wars and just gives you what it was like point-blank.

the garfield movie(real)

The Garfield Movie
I’m aware this may seem like a meme entry, but I do believe that the Garfield movie has some redeeming qualities to it. I personally enjoyed its mature theme of child abandonment, and how it makes sure there's things for the adults and the kids alike.

Checkmate Lincolnites! Finale
I’m not sure if this counts or not, as it barely scores an hour long, but I still enjoyed it. It has both serious topics, and witty jokes, while still maintaining a status of being used as a reference video. I must also point out that this movie is NOT pro-Confederacy, rather against it.
