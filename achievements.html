Being absolutely CRACKED at Seterra

For those who are unacquainted, Seterra is a game or rather a series of games where your objective is to identify what country is being specified. For example, you are given the prompt of Switzerland, and in front of you is a map of Europe. From there you must find the geographical location of Switzerland. There are other gamemodes and other maps but this is the main idea of it.

I consider myself good at this game as I've filled out all of Europe before in under a minute.
